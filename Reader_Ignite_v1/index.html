Steps to play a Learning Resource

1. Click on the Activity icon situated at the top left-hand corner of
    the e-book.

2. A pop-up for the selected Learning Resource will appear on the
   screen.

Steps to use TOC

1. The TOC icon displays the Table of Contents in the e-book.

2. Click on the name of a chapter or its starting page number to go
    directly to the chapter.

Steps to add a Bookmark

1. Click on the Bookmark icon in the top panel of the e-book.

2. Click on the Add Bookmark tab.

3. A yellow tag will appear at the top right-hand corner of the page
    like the one below.

4. Click on the tag. A star will appear on the tag to show that the
    bookmark is saved.

Steps to delete a bookmark

To delete a bookmark, click on the star that appears on the tag.

Steps to Highlight Text

1. Select the text you would like to highlight using the cursor.

2. Click on the Highlight icon in the top panel of the e-book.

3. Select a colour of your choice from the drop-down box to
    highlight your text.

Steps to Remove the Highlight

1. To delete the highlight, simply click on the highlighted portion of
    the text.

2. A pop-up will appear, asking if you wish to delete the highlight.

3. Click on ‘Yes’ if you want to delete the highlight.

2. Select a colour of your choice from the drop-down box to highlight

Steps to go to any Page

1. Click on the Go to Page dialog box in the top panel of the
    e-book.

2. Type in the page number you would like to access and press
    ‘Enter’. The selected page will be shown.

Steps to use the Search Feature

1. Click on the Search icon in the top panel of the e-book.

2. Enter the keyword in the dialog box. Click on the Search icon on
    the right.

3. A list containing all sentences in which the specified keyword is
    present, and their page numbers, is displayed.

Steps to Change between Single and Double Views

To change the mode of the page you are currently viewing, click on the View icon in the top panel of the e-book.

Steps to use Glossary

1. Click on the Glossary icon in the toolbar.

2. A complete glossary of difficult words from the book is displayed.

3. You can use the Search keyword option to search for a particular
   difficult word in the book.

Steps to use Audio Speed

1. Click on the Audio Speed icon in the toolbar.

2. A slider to change audio playback speed is displayed.

3. You can move the slider left or right to decrease or increase the book audio playback speed.

Steps to use the Pen Tool

1. Click on the Pen icon, the annotation toolbox gets visible.

2. The pen can be used to make annotations or any marks on the
    ebook.

3. The toolbox also has an eraser to erase the marks made by the
    pen and an undo button to undo any markings.

4. Pen and eraser icons work in a toggle mode.

Steps to use Zoom-In/Zoom-Out

1. Click on the Zoom-In icon to enlarge size of text so that it
    appears larger or nearer which can zoom upto 4 times the
    original size.

2. Click on the Zoom-Out icon to reduce the size of text so that it
    appears smaller and further away.

3. A zoom operation just changes the size of the font on-screen
    without impacting the content in any way. Also the page can be
    dragged to focus on any specific portion of the content.

Steps to use the Help Feature

Click on the Help icon to know how to use the features of the various icons.

More Displays Hidden Buttons

This button is for viewing more tools which help in making the ebook user-friendly.

Steps to Insert a Note

1. Click on the Note icon in the top panel of the e-book.

2. Click on the Add Note tab.

3. Click on the page where you want the note to be placed.

4. Enter the text for your note and click on Save.

5. Your note would appear as the icon below.

6. The note gets saved and can be read whenever the page is opened.


Steps to Delete a Note

1. Click on the icon.

2. A pop-up containing the information in the note will appear.

3. Click on the button at the top right-hand corner of the note.

4. A pop-up will appear, asking if you wish to delete the note.

5. Click on ‘Yes’ if you want to delete the note.

Steps to use Thumbnail

1. Click on the Thumbnail icon in the toolbar.

2. The pages of the chapter will show up as thumbnails.

3. Clicking on a thumbnail will take the user directly to the page
    he/she wants to go to.

Steps to use Spotlight

1. Click on the Spotlight icon in the toolbar.

2. A resizable circular window appears and only portion of the
    content that fits into the circle will be visible.

3. The rest of the view of the page is hidden/masked.

4. The spotlight can be dragged/increased/decreased in size to
    focus on a specific area.

Steps to use Read Mode

1. Click on the Read Mode icon in the top panel of the e-book.

2. A pop-up window opens on the page, displaying the page content
    in a bigger font size for better readability.

3. Different sections in the chapter are shown either in white or pale
    background to differentiate these from each other.

Steps to use Masking Feature

1. Click on the Mask icon in the toolbar.

2. A resizable rectangular black box will appear on the ebook and
    hide content.

3. The teacher could use this feature to hide a portion of the page
    while asking students any question related to the content.

4. The mask can be dragged/increased/decreased in size to focus on
   a specific area.

Steps to View/hide the On-page Audio Icons

On clicking this toggle button, the audio icons on the page/s will be viewed or hidden.

Steps to use Toolbar Expand/Collapse

1. This will help expand and collapse the toolbar.

2. Collapse hides the toolbar, allowing more space for the page display.
